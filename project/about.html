About Us

This website is built by Shrinketh Deevenapalli and Kushagra Singh as a utility hub for BITS Hyderabad students. The idea is to simplify access to campus resources, information, and services.

Our aim is to bring commonly used details like the mess menu, contacts, and map together in one place with a clean, responsive interface.

Feel free to reach out for suggestions or contributions!
